Life on the Farm

A Ranch of One's Own

So, you've decided to take over the old farm. Step one is to roll up your sleeves and get to work! Till your fields and water the crops to harvest produce, then raise livestock to collect byproducts. Ship the fruits (and veggies) of your labor out to market, and all that hard work will pay off!

Know Thy Neighbors

Just like growing crops, cultivating relationships with the residents of Mineral Town takes time. Chat with the townsfolk and give them their favorite gifts to watch your bonds bloom into beautiful friendships! And you never know—that boy or girl you like just might return your feelings!

Time Flies When You're Running a Farm

There's lots to do while you're out and about, and daylight's burning! The residents of Mineral Town each follow a unique schedule and open or close their businesses accordingly. Just remember that you won't recover Stamina if you stay up too late at night, so make sure to get plenty of rest for the day ahead!

The Changing of the Seasons

Each day consists of 24 hours, every week lasts seven days, there are 30 days in a given season, and the year ends after all four seasons (Spring → Summer → Autumn → Winter) have passed. The crops you can raise will vary from season to season, with one exception: you can't plant anything in winter!
