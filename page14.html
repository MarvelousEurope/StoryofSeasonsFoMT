Town Facilities （1）

Mineral Town Southside

Southside is the entrance to Mineral Town. Yodel Ranch, PoPoultry, and the Forge are all situated here.

1The Forge
With the requisite gold and materials, Saibara will upgrade your leveled-up farming tools. He also sells tools, accessories, and Makers.
2PoPoultry
Lillia buys and sells chickens and Angora rabbits. You can also purchase Feed and Breeding Kits from her.
3Mineral Beach
Huang offers imported seeds, toys to play with your pets on the beach, and other curiosities. Also, Kai opens his Beach Cafe every summer to sell cool drinks and seasonal snacks.
4Rose Plaza
A gathering place for the townsfolk, as well as the site of many a festival. Check the bulletin board to stay up to date with the latest news and upcoming events!
5Yodel Ranch
Mugi buys and sells cows, sheep, and alpacas. You can also purchase Fodder and Breeding Kits from him.
