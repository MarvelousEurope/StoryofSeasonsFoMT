Menu Guide

The Field Screen

1Stamina
Your Stamina is displayed in both a numerical value (current/total) and as Power Berries. When you use a tool, the Power Berries and the numerical Stamina value will decrease. As you run out of Stamina, you will begin to build up Fatigue. Recover Stamina and get rid of Fatigue by taking a dip in the Hot Spring or by getting a good night's sleep.
2Fatigue
Using tools after losing Stamina or working late into the night will cause your Fatigue to build. The face icon changes as you become more fatigued, in the order of →→→. If you keep working while you're blue in the face, you'll pass out and get sent to the Clinic! Always keep an eye on your Fatigue, and make sure you get enough sleep to recover from it!
3Tool Pocket
The highlighted icon in the center indicates the tool you currently have equipped.
4Date
Displays the season, the date, and whether a festival is being held that day.
5Weather & Time
Displays the weather (sunny, rainy, snowy, etc.) and the time on a 12-hour clock.
6Gold (G)
Displays your current funds.
7Bag
The icon indicates which item you are currently holding.

The Town Map & Farm Map

Press the + Button to view the Town Map or the - Button to bring up your Farm Map. If you're ever lost, are looking for a certain shop, or need to track down your stray animals, never fear! Your map will always be there!

The Town Map

Check your position and surrounding locations on the Town Map. Hover your cursor over an area and press the Y Button to check a selected shop's hours of operation.

The Farm Map

Check your position and your animals' locations on the Farm Map. The crops and trees you currently have planted in the field will also be displayed as icons on the map.

The Menu Screen

Pressing the X Button on the field screen opens the Menu Screen. Flip through the various submenus by pressing the L and R Buttons.

Settings Game Settings: Change volume, adjust display settings, and toggle the rumble feature.
Button Presets: Choose your preferred button setup from three preset control schemes.
Farm Information View your status as a farmer, field statistics, and currently owned animals and materials.
Items View, move, eat, or throw away the items you currently have stored inside your Bag.
Journal Save your game by selecting "Write in Journal," or load a previous save by selecting "Read Journal."
Tool Pocket View the tools in your inventory, or press the + Button to display a list of your tools' current levels.
Map View the Town Map and Farm Map.
Relationships View your relationships with the town residents, the Nature Sprites, your livestock, and your pets.

Organizing Items

To move and switch items or tools around your inventory, all you have to do is choose an object, press the A Button, and place it wherever you'd like! You can also press the X Button to move a single item or tool, or an individual item from a stack, to an empty slot.

Throwing Away/Eating Items

Throw away an item you don't need by highlighting it in your inventory and pressing the + Button. Edible items can be eaten with the Y Button. Items that recover Stamina and/or Fatigue will display their recovery values next to the Fatigue and Stamina meters. Items that reduce Fatigue when eaten display their value with a minus sign (which is good in that case!). Upon selecting an item stack, you can choose how many items from that stack to throw away or eat.
