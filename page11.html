Farm Facilities

Let's take a look at the facilities you'll be able to use on your farm!

The Shipping Bin

Place crops, byproducts, ores, and more in any of your three Shipping Bins, and Zack will visit your farm at 5:00 PM daily (excluding holidays) to collect your goods and pay you accordingly.

Shipping Basics

While examining a Shipping Bin, you can move items to and from your Bag. You can also throw in items that you're currently holding by examining the Bin with the A Button. Again, though, be aware: once an item's in the Bin, there's no getting it back!

Your Mailbox

Check your Mailbox whenever you see a letter icon—you might find letters from the townsfolk, notifications of upcoming events, or ads for new products! Any correspondence you receive will automatically be stored in your Bookshelf for future reference.

Your Barn & Coop

Your Barn is for housing cows, sheep, and alpacas. You also have a Coop to house chickens and Angora rabbits. Both have a Shipping Bin, as well as stalls for placing Fodder or Feed. Outside either facility is a Bell. Give those Bells a ring on a sunny day to call your animals out to pasture!

Your Silo & Water Mill

You can store Fodder in your Silo, which is found next to your Barn. Next to your Coop is a Water Mill, which can turn Corn into Feed for your chickens and Angora rabbits. When you save Fodder or Feed, you can check how much of it you have in each storage container by examining the sign near the Dispenser (the red box).

The Fish Pond

You can save and place the fish you've caught here. Examine the pond while holding a fish to release it into its new home. It's also a handy spot for refilling your Watering Can.
