Starting the Game

Title Screen

The Title Screen is where it all begins. Just choose an option to get playing!

Continue Load a saved game. Only available if you have an existing save.
Start Customize your character, then begin a new farming adventure.
Options Change the display language: English, French, German, or Spanish.
Manual Open the online manual to learn more details about the game.
About Saving

You can save your progress by examining the Journal in your house, or by selecting the "Journal" menu. You can save up to eight files, and there will always be a single auto-save file. Auto-saves occur upon eating breakfast in the morning and will overwrite the auto-save file.
