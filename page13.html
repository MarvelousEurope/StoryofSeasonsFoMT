Around Town

Talking with the Townsfolk

Mineral Town is full of friendly faces! Get to know the townsfolk by speaking with them and giving them presents. Choose an item with the Right Stick, then hand it over with the A Button.

Friendship & Love

Interacting with and giving gifts to the townsfolk will increase their friendship with you, while doing the same with marriage candidates will raise their affection for you. Foster a loving friendship, and soon you'll be able to start dating, get married, have a child, and experience other events!

Going Shopping

Shops like the General Store and the Forge are available in town. Depending on the store, you can purchase items, animals, and food.

Local Events

Mineral Town holds various yearly events. During some events, you might receive gifts from marriage candidates with high Friendship Points or Love Points. There are also events where you'll be able to let your livestock and pets participate in competitions! Keep an eye out for them as the seasons change.
