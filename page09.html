Farm, Sweet Farm (1)

From raising livestock to growing crops to owning pets, there's so much you can do on your farm!

Raising Crops

Grow crops by tilling your field, planting some seeds, and watering them daily. Once your crops are ready for harvest, you can ship them, gift them, or use them to whip up a home-cooked meal!

（1） Prepare the Field

First, you'll want to clear your field of debris. You can pick up and throw away Weeds, Stones, and Branches, but you'll need an improved Axe to chop up stumps and an improved Hammer to get rid of large rocks. Once you've got some open ground to work with, you can use your Hoe to till some squares of farmable land!

（2） Sowing Seeds

When you equip seeds, you'll see their planting range, which shows you where the seeds will fall on tilled land. Once you've found the perfect spot to plant them, let it sow!

Each seed type can only be planted during its own season. Sowing out of season will not yield any crops at all, so be sure you've got the right seeds before you sow!

*You can buy seeds at various shops across Mineral Town.

*You cannot sow seeds where others are already planted.

（3） Water Your Crops

Once you've sown some seeds, all you have to do is equip your Watering Can and give 'em a nice drink of water! Keep your crops healthy by watering them once a day, every day. Don't forget, or your garden won't grow!

The Watering Can icon displays how much water you have left. If the meter runs empty, you can refill it at your Fish Pond or at Mineral Beach.

*No need to worry about watering any crops on rainy days!

（4） Harvesting

Once your crops have fully grown, stand in front of a crop and press the A Button to harvest it. After being harvested, the field will typically revert to its regular tilled state.

Some crops, such as Cucumbers and Tomatoes, will continue to grow even after your first harvest. Keep watering them, and they'll produce multiple yields throughout the season!
