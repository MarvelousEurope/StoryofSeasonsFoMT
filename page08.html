Home, Sweet Home

The furniture in your house allows you to get some rest, save your progress, store items for later, and much more! Some furniture has to be purchased first, but once you buy it, you'll have access to it whenever you come home!

Your Bed

Long day? Get some rest! After hitting the hay, you'll wake up the next morning with more Stamina and lower Fatigue.

Your Journal

You can save your progress by selecting "Write in Journal," or you can load a previous save by selecting "Read Journal."

The Calendar

Consult your Calendar to keep up to date! If you see on a certain date, that means it's someone's birthday! Festival days are marked by and can be selected to view more details pertaining to that festival.

The Bookshelf

Over the course of your farming adventure, your Bookshelf will store all the letters and tutorials you've read along the way, as well as your balance sheets and other statistics.

The Television

Your TV may be tiny, but it's got all the channels you need! You can watch any one of these programs:

Weather The weather forecast fills you in on what to expect from Mother Nature tomorrow.
Education Hear fun facts and answers to frequently asked questions about life on the farm!
Entertainment A different show airs each day of the week. Tune in to learn recipes or just veg out!
News If an event is being held soon, you can watch the news to find out more about it.

The Mirror

Look in the Mirror to change your outfit. Whether you choose to wear overalls or a hoodie, you'll be farming in style!

Cabinet & Tool Box

Store items* from your Bag or Tool Pocket in these. Press the L or R Buttons to swap between inventory and storage space. *Crops, food, and other perishables must be stored in a Fridge.

Upgrading Your Home

As time goes by, you will have the option to renovate your house. The bigger it's built, the more furniture you'll be able to purchase and place in your home. Ask Gotts for an estimate at his Workshop, and he'll tell you how much material and gold is required for the job.
