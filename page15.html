Town Facilities （2）

Mineral Town Northside

Most of Mineral Town's residents call Northside their home, making it a natural hot spot for various shops and services.

1The Library
Basil is happy to share his writings about agriculture and the town. If you have a question, take a look in a book!
2General Store
Purchase seeds, ingredients, and other items to make your life a little easier. Jeff's stock will change depending on the season.
3Adge Winery
Duke ferments and bottles the grapes he harvests from his Vineyard, which his wife Manna sells as their signature Grape Juice.
4Nature Sprites' Hut
If you regularly give the Nature Sprites presents and play games with them, they'll be happy to help you with your farm work!
5The Church
If you're feeling spiritual, come here to listen to one of Carter's sermons, or repent your sins at the Confessional.
6Mineral Clinic
Elly sells medicines that can be used to recover Stamina and relieve Fatigue, while Doctor provides medical checkups.
7The Inn
You already have a place to sleep, but you can purchase fortifying meals and beverages from Dudley throughout the day.
