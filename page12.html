Facilities Near the Farm

The Hot Spring

Need a break from all that fieldwork? Take a dip to restore Stamina and recover from Fatigue. If you're in need of a quick snack, throw an Egg in the spring to boil yourself a Hot Spring Egg!

Spring Mine & Lake Mine

In the mines, you can dig up items or smash rocks to find ore. Uncover a ladder, and you can descend into the lower floors. The deeper you go, the more valuable treasures you'll find!

The Workshop

Here you can buy furniture from Gotts the carpenter, or commission him to upgrade the buildings on your farm. Just make sure you've got enough gold and materials for the job!

Mother's Hill

Mother's Hill is a mountain near your farm where you can gather flowers, mushrooms, and other natural resources. The peak offers a breathtaking view.
