Performing Actions

Movement

Tilting the Left Stick fully in one direction lets you run, while tilting it gently will allow you to walk.

Auto-Run & Sidestepping

If you hold down the R Button, you will automatically run in the direction you're currently facing. Change direction while auto-running by tilting the Left Stick. You can also tilt the Left Stick while holding the ZR Button to sidestep, facing in a fixed direction while moving sideways or backwards.

Talking & Examining

Press the A Button while close to a resident or object to speak with or examine them. You can also use the A Button to pick up nearby items.

Holding & Using Items

Tilt the Right Stick to choose and hold an item from your Bag. While holding it, you can give it to someone by talking to them, or examine one of your farm's Shipping Bins to sell the item for gold. (Be aware: once you put an item in the Shipping Bin, you can't take it back out!) Press the B Button to put the item you're holding back in your Bag. If the item you're carrying is edible, you can press the Y Button to eat it.

Using Tools

Use the left and right Directional Buttons to equip one of your tools, then press the Y Button to use it. Using a tool will decrease your Stamina and build up Fatigue. Holding the Y Button with a tool equipped will charge it up, but only after you've upgraded that tool at the Forge.

Upgrading Your Tools

The more you use a tool, the more experience you'll gain with it. After you've sufficiently leveled up a tool and have gathered the required ore, take them both to the Forge for an upgrade. Once the blacksmith works his magic, you'll be able to water more crops, break bigger boulders, till your fields faster, and more!

Gone Fishin'!

When you have a Fishing Rod equipped, you can use it near a body of water to cast your line and bobber out. If you're patient (and lucky), your bobber will begin to move! You'll know you've got a bite if you see a "!" icon. Press the Y Button to reel in your catch, and if your timing is right, you'll fish it right up!
